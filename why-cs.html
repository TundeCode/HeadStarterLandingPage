Why I chose to get into software development

Since childhood, I have aspired to build my own game, a goal I continue to pursue. My interest in computers and technology has been a lifelong passion. It wasn't until my senior year of high school that I took my first computer science class. Using code.org, I created my very first project: an application that allows users to select an NBA division and view all the teams within that division.

From that day forward, I fell in love with developing my own applications and projects. I realized that this field allows me to channel my creativity, and I am driven by the ability to bring my ideas to life through coding. It is a career path I am passionate about and eager to continue pursuing.
